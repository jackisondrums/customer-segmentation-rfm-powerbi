# Business Findings

## Key Insights

- Loyal VIPs make up one of the largest customer groups.
- Champions represent highly valuable customers with strong frequency and monetary scores.
- At Risk customers may need re-engagement campaigns.
- Potential Loyalists could be targeted with loyalty offers.

## Recommendations

- Reward Champions with exclusive offers.
- Send retention campaigns to At Risk customers.
- Encourage Potential Loyalists to purchase more frequently.
- Use customer segments to create targeted marketing campaigns.
